Van Gogh’s Palettes

The five most dominant colors from 913 of Vincent van Gogh’s oil and watercolor paintings. Images and metadata were sourced from this Wikpedia list, which is available under a Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported License license. Data was scraped and processed using Python, with help from this blog post on finding dominant image colors. The algorithm uses K-Means clustering in order to extract the n-most dominant colors (I ran it for the top five).
